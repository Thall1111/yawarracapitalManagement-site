Sovereign Governance. Cultural Authority. Stewardship.

Yawarra Cultural Custodian Council (YCCC) & Yawarra Capital Management (YCM) — embedding cultural law, Elder approvals, and intergenerational benefit in every project.

Mission

Safeguarding Indigenous sovereignty through CAF & FPIC, certifying projects that protect Country, respect culture, and create long-term community value.

Vision

A future where Indigenous sovereignty guides Australia’s transition to a just and sustainable economy.

Differentiators

  • Dual Rail Governance — Cultural sovereignty + fiduciary law.
  • Sovereign Certification — CAF ensures legitimacy and Elder approval.
  • Scalable Platform — Funds, SPVs, and institutional partnerships.
  • Traceability — All approvals logged, audit-ready.
