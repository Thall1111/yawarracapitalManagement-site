Sovereign Capital for a Nature-Positive Future.

51% Indigenous-owned, AFSL-compliant. Delivering Dual Alpha - Cultural & Financial.

Mission

To accelerate Australia’s transition to a nature-positive economy by pairing sovereign Indigenous governance with institutional fiduciary discipline. We regenerate land, restore water, decarbonise industry, and create auditable shared value for communities and investors.

Vision

Enable Indigenous sovereignty as foundation for Australia’s next economy by embedding cultural authority into climate finance, infrastructure investment and Indigenous enterprise. Majority Indigenous-owned through the Cultural Custodian Council (CCC) and formally approved by Wiradjuri Elders, YCM partners with Frontier Impact Group (FIG, AFSL trustee) to deliver Australia’s first Indigenous-majority, AFSL-aligned fund platform.

Differentiators

  • Dual Rail Governance: Sovereign governance (CCC, ORIC) + fiduciary governance (ASIC, AFSL).
  • Sovereign Certification: CAF certification eliminates black-cladding risk.
  • Scalable Platform: From pooled funds (YCM, YARNT) to project-level SPVs.
  • Traceability & Audit: Approvals, decisions and certifications logged with integrity controls.
